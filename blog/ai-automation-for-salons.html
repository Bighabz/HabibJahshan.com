5. Appointment Reminders and Confirmations Saves 1 hour/week

No-shows and last-minute cancellations cost salons thousands per month. The best way to prevent them is automated reminders.

What AI does:

  • 48 hours before: "Hi! Just confirming your appointment with Maria on Thursday at 2pm. Reply YES to confirm or CANCEL if you need to reschedule."
  • Day of: "See you in 2 hours! If you're running late, just reply and let us know."

If someone cancels, the AI can instantly offer that time slot to your waitlist.

Results:

  • No-show rate drops from 15-20% to 3-5%
  • For a busy salon doing 80 appointments/week, that's 8-12 fewer no-shows per week
  • At $80/appointment, that's $640-960/week in saved revenue

Time saved: No more manually texting reminders the night before.
